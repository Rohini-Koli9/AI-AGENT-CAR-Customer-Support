# Maruti Suzuki Warranty and CCP Policies

## Standard Warranty

All new Maruti Suzuki vehicles come with a standard manufacturer warranty of **3 years or 100,000 kilometers**, whichever comes first.

### Standard Warranty Coverage:
- Manufacturing defects
- Mechanical failures (engine, transmission, drivetrain)
- Electrical system failures
- Original equipment components

### Standard Warranty Exclusions:
- Regular maintenance and servicing
- Wear and tear items (brake pads, clutch plates, tires, batteries)
- Damage from accidents or misuse
- Modifications or unauthorized repairs
- Cosmetic damage

- Vehicle should not be started after water entry

**Claim Requirements:**

- Fuel station receipt (if available)
- Report damage within 48 hours
- Do not add fresh fuel before inspection

#### 3. Rodent Damage
**Coverage:**
- Wiring harness damage caused by rodents

- Insulation damage to electrical components
- Sensor wire damage

**Claim Requirements:**
- Evidence of rodent activity
- Photographs of damaged components
- Report within 7 days of discovery

**Coverage Limit:** Up to â‚¹15,000 per incident

#### 4. Insect Damage
**Coverage:**
- ECU damage from insect infestation

**Claim Requirements:**

## Claim Filing Process

### Step 1: Incident Reporting
- Report incident immediately (within specified timeframes)
- Call customer support: 1800-102-1800
- Provide vehicle registration and warranty details
- Initial assessment over phone

### Step 2: Service Center Visit
- Visit nearest authorized Maruti Suzuki service center
- Bring required documents:
  * Vehicle registration certificate
  * Extended Warranty documents
  * CCP coverage documents
  * Photo ID proof
  * Incident photographs (if available)

### Step 3: Inspection
- Service center inspects vehicle
- Damage assessment by authorized technician
- Photographs and detailed report created
- **Timeline:** Within 24-48 hours

### Step 4: Claim Submission
- Service center submits claim to warranty department
- Claim reference number provided to customer
- Estimated cost of repair calculated

### Step 5: Claim Evaluation
- Warranty department reviews claim
- Verifies coverage under CCP terms
- Checks for policy exclusions
- **Timeline:** 3-5 business days

### Step 6: Approval/Rejection
- Customer notified of decision
- If approved: Repair work authorized
- If rejected: Detailed reason provided
- **Timeline:** 5-7 business days total

### Step 7: Repair Completion
- Authorized repairs completed
- Quality check performed
- Vehicle returned to customer
- Claim closed

### Claim Rejection Reasons
Common reasons for CCP claim rejection:
1. Damage type not covered under CCP
2. Incident occurred outside CCP validity period
3. Vehicle mileage exceeded package limit
4. Damage from excluded cause (accident, misuse)
5. Required documentation not provided
6. Evidence of pre-existing damage
7. Unauthorized repairs attempted
8. Service history not maintained

- Extended Warranty can be transferred to new owner
- Transfer fee: â‚¹1,000

- Transfer request must be submitted within 30 days of vehicle sale

- Extended Warranty cancellation as per refund policy
- CCP cancellation only within 15 days of purchase

### Automatic Cancellation
Warranty/CCP automatically cancelled if:
- Vehicle is declared total loss
- Vehicle sold for scrap
- Odometer tampering detected
- Major accident with frame damage
